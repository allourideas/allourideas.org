What is this?

All Our Ideas is a platform that enables groups to collect and prioritise ideas in a transparent, democratic, and bottom-up way. It’s a suggestion box for the digital age.

Learn more

How does it work?

You can use All Our Ideas to create a website where visitors can vote on ideas and upload new ones. The intuitive and fun voting process yields powerful results.
