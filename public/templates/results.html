About the Scoring

The score of an idea is the estimated chance that it will win against a randomly chosen idea. For example, a score of 100 means the idea is predicted to win every time and a score of 0 means the idea is predicted to lose every time.

About word clouds

The word clouds displayed on this page are a visual depiction of the words that most frequently appear in this idea marketplace. When weighted by score, the generated word cloud will use the current score of an idea to determine the popularity of the word. Note that some common or infrequent words may be filtered out of the generated word cloud.
